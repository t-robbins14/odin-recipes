Recipe for Banana Pancakes

Home

Description

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • One Egg
  • Milk
  • Vegtable Oil
  • Bananas

Steps to Make

  1. Mix dry Ingredients(flour, sugar, salt, baking powder) in one bowl.
  2. Mix wet Ingredients(one egg, milk, vegtable oil) in another bowl.
  3. Mash or leave out bananas for now, it's up to you!
  4. If mashed, add them to the wet Ingredients bowl.
  5. Put the dry and wet Ingredients all in one bowl.
  6. Stir them up!
  7. Then pour the batter in 1/4 cup portions on an oil pan or griddle.
  8. Cook them on medium heat, and flip them with a spatula every few minutes
  9. Cook until both sides are a golden brown color!
  10. Put them on a plate, if you didn't mash, put your sliced bananas on the pancake!
  11. Enjoy your meal!
