Recipe for Chocolate Chip Cookies

Home

Description

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps to Make

  1. Smash butter and sugar and do the same to the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Put them in a bowl.
  3. Let the baking soda dissolve in hot water and then add it to the above bowl.
  4. Put the flour and chocolate chips in to the bowl.
  5. Stir it all up!
  6. Put the stirred dough onto a baking sheet.
  7. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  8. Once it's heated, put the baking sheet into the oven!
  9. Wait at least 10 minutes.
  10. Cook until the edges of the cookies are a golden brown color.
  11. Take them out and let them cool off.
  12. Enjoy your meal!
