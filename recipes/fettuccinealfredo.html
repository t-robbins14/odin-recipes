Recipe for Fettuccine Alfredo

Home

Description

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine Pasta
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Salt
  • Romano and Parmesan Cheese

Steps to Make

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove.
  3. Add the seasonings(salt, pepper, garlic salt) to it and stir in the cheese.
  4. Put the pasta in what you stirred.
  5. Wait for it to cool a little.
  6. Enjoy your meal!
